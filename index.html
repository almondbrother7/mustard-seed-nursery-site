About Us

The Mustard Seed Nursery is a small, family-run grower of edible, native, healthy survival / victory garden plants in Brevard County, Florida. We sell healthy, chemical-free plants rooted in faith and good soil. We also have a selection of native plants for attracting butterflies.

Victory Garden / Native-Plant Survival Garden

  • Banana and Plantain Trees
  • Longevity Spinach
  • Sweet Potatoes
  • Cassava (Yuca root)
  • Pigeon Peas

Butterfly Garden

  • Blue Porter Weed
  • Coreopsis (FL state wildflower)
  • Milkweed
  • Lantana
  • Passionflower
