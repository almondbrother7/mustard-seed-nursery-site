About Us

The Mustard Seed Nursery is a small, family-run grower of edible, native, and spiritually symbolic plants in Brevard County, Florida. We sell healthy, chemical-free plants rooted in faith and good soil.

Where to Find Us

Visit us weekly at the Brevard County Farmers Market, held at the UF/IFAS Extension Office.

  • Address: 3695 Lake Dr, Cocoa, FL
  • Wednesdays: 3–6 PM

Current Plants

  • Fig trees
  • Sweet basil
  • Mustard greens (heirloom)
  • Passionflower vines
